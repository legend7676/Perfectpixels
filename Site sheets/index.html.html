About Me

Hi, I’m Sadiq baba, a professional photographer with a deep passion for capturing authentic moments and turning them into timeless memories. For over 5 years, I’ve specialized in portrait, event, and lifestyle photography — blending creativity with precision to tell powerful visual stories. Whether I’m shooting a wedding, a personal branding session, or a serene landscape, my goal is always the same: to reflect the genuine emotion, personality, and beauty of each subject through my lens.
Based in KANO, I work with clients locally and internationally, offering a warm, collaborative experience from the first meeting to the final edit. Photography, to me, isn’t just about taking pictures — it’s about preserving life’s most meaningful chapters. Let’s create something unforgettable together.
